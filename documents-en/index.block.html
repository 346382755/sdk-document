Why use Giant Mobile SDK.

    In China, there are a good number of android app store providers, all of which have account systems like facebook and the payment systems like google play. This will cause lots of troubles for the game developers. But our Giant Mobile SDK will save your troubles by simply having you access to our interface without extra development workload.
